MTL MLOpt


Montréal Machine Learning and Optimization (MTL MLOpt) is a group of researchers living and working in Montréal, whose research loosely spans topics in machine learning and mathematical optimization. Most, though not all, members are affiliated with the Mila, where we also hold our biweekly meetings. The group includes researchers from the University of Montréal, McGill, Google Brain, Samsung SAIT AI Lab (SAIL) Montreal, Facebook AI Research Montréal (FAIR) and Microsoft Research Montréal.

Our meetings typically comprise of a presentation of a member's latest work, followed by discussion which often leads to productive collaborations. Our meetings are open to the public. Join our mailing group to receive the latest meeting announcements.
